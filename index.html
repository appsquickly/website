    Our Process

    Our engagement model is refreshingly simple!

    • Get in touch with us

      Get in touch with us to discuss your idea or development requirements.

    • Feedback

      We'll work with you to establish product backlog and mitigate any technical risks that we foresee. At this stage we'll publish daily builds, so that you can give us feedback as the application begins to take shape.

    • Release Plan

      Once the scope has become clear, we'll prepare a quote for the implementation of the project. Alternatively, you may wish to continue on a time and materials basis, which allows re-prioritization of features based on feedback (we'll conduct your team's weekly iteration planning and retrospective sessions).
