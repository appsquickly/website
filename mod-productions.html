ACO Virtual : Mod Productions

An interactive touring installation featuring the Australian Chamber Orchestra.

The brief

Mod Productions in collaboration with the Australian Chamber Orchestra endeavored to create a touring installation that would allow people to experience the orchestra in a unique way. The grand plan included 3D video footage and multi-track audio that can be re-mixed in real-time by the audience. The iOS development portion of this ambitious project was awarded to AppsQuick.ly, with the responsibility to smoothly transition ongoing maintenance duties to their in-house developers at the completion of the project.

The results

This immersive experience won numerous awards, including 'AppStore Best New Apps'. Michela Ledwidge of Mod Productions remarked that iPad Controller and companion augmented reality application for iPhone were the most stable applications that they'd had developed to date.

Project details

  • Date: December 2012 to March 2013
  • Client: Mod Productions
  • What we Used: iOS SDKs, Typhoon Framework, Open Sound Control, OpenSource libraries
